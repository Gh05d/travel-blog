Trade Routes That Transformed Cultures

Throughout history, famous trade routes like the Silk Road and the Incense Route shaped more than just commerce‚Äîthey connected civilizations. The Silk Road, stretching from China to the Mediterranean, featured walkable sections through historic towns and desert caravan paths.
