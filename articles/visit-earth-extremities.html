Climbers face unpredictable weather, oxygen shortages, and the danger of avalanches or icefall collapses. Preparing for such an expedition requires careful coordination and resilience. Many hire experienced Sherpas who guide and support them through treacherous sections.

Reaching the summit is not only a personal achievement but also a symbol of human endurance. However, environmental concerns grow with each season.
