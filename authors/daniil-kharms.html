Daniil Kharms

Daniil grew up hiking the Caucasus with his father and hasn't stopped moving since. He's summited peaks on five continents, surfed both coasts of Central America, and spent three months tracking wildlife across Botswana's Okavango Delta.

His writing covers hiking, surfing, and safari travel, with honest assessments of difficulty, fitness requirements, and what the brochures don't mention. He's not interested in bucket-list bragging—just helping people find adventures that match their actual ability.

Based in Tbilisi. Sleeps better in a tent than a hotel.
